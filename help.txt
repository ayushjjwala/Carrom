To run the game just type './carrom' 


Rules:

1) Use Mouse drag or 'left' or 'right' arrow keys to move the striker initially.
2) Use 'up' and 'down' arrow keys to set power of the striker.
3) use 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' to move the aim which is initially present at the center of the board.
4) Press 'space bar' to launch the striker.
5) You get +10 for every black coin that get into the pocket and -5 for every white coin goes in the pocket.
6) The game ends when you pocket all the black coins.
